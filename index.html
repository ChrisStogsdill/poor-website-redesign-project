About Us

China House offers delicious dining and delivery to Oklahoma City, OK.

China House is a cornerstone in the Oklahoma City community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff.

Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
